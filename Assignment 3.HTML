Bread Butter Toast - Childhood Breakfast

"This is a recipe for deliciously rich bread butter toast, and it became my favorite breakfast because of its warm, crispy comfort."

Ingredients:

  • Bread slices (2-4)
  • Butter (as needed)
  • Sugar or honey (optional)

Recipe:

  1. Take 2 slices of bread.
  2. Spread butter evenly on one or both sides.
  3. Heat a frying pan or tawa on low flame.
  4. Place the buttered bread on the pan.
  5. Toast each side for 1-2 minutes until golden and crispy.
